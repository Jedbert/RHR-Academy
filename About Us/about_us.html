ABOUT US

Welcome to Respectech-HR Academy

Respectech-HR Academy (R-HR) We are a leading institution dedicated to providing comprehensive and industry-relevant education and training in the field of technology. We have undergone a rebranding to reflect our specialized focus on technology education and career development. Our academy offers a wide range of programs and courses designed to meet the needs of students at various skill levels, from beginners to professionals seeking to advance their tech careers in Cybersecurity, Cloud Computing, Data Science, Digital Forensics and more.
Our aim is to upskill Nigerian graduates with relevant marketable skills, including digital marketing, graphic design, web development, product development, data management, and other technological skills.
Additionally, assistance will be provided to trained youths to access gainful employment through our HR services in local and international companies.

Our Mission | Vision | Values

Our mission is to bridge the skills gap and empower individuals to thrive in the digital era by providing them with high-quality education, practical training, and industry certifications. We strive to equip our students with the knowledge, skills, and confidence necessary to succeed in their chosen tech careers.

Our vision is to be a leading institution in technology education, recognized for our innovative approach, industry-aligned programs, and the success of our graduates. We aim to contribute to the growth and development of the tech industry by nurturing talented professionals who can make a positive impact in their workplaces and society as a whole.

Respectech-HR Core values Innovation, dependable technology, equality and diversity, corporate social responsibility, philanthropies, quality, transparency, teamwork, continuous learning. Innovation is key to staying ahead of the competition in this industry and creating new products or services that set the company apart.
